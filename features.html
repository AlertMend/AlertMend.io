Main Features

Drive Operational Efficiency, Reduce Costs, and Scale Seamlessly with Automated Incident Management for Kubernetes Environments

Pre-built Remediation Flows (RFs)

Automate up to 90% of incident responses in Kubernetes environments. Our pre-built remediation flows are optimized for AKS, EKS, GKE, and self-hosted clusters, ensuring quick resolutions without manual intervention.

Customizable Workflows

Tailor workflows specifically for your Kubernetes infrastructure. With AlertMend, you can design workflows that respond to your cluster's unique needs, improving efficiency and response times.

Cross-Cluster Command Execution

Manage multiple Kubernetes clusters—whether AKS, EKS, GKE, or self-hosted—from a single platform. Execute commands across clusters to streamline operations and ensure consistency across your fleet.

Integration with Popular Alerting Tools

AlertMend integrates seamlessly with alerting tools like Prometheus Alertmanager and Grafana to automate incident responses in Kubernetes environments. Set triggers for alerts and let the system handle the rest.

Proactive Incident Prevention (Status Page Crawling)

Proactively manage Kubernetes cluster dependencies by crawling third-party status pages and detecting issues before they impact your services. AlertMend helps you stay ahead of potential disruptions.

Secure Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

Protect your Kubernetes environment with role-based access control, ensuring that only authorized personnel can access critical workflows and resources.

Multi-Cloud and Hybrid Kubernetes Support

Whether you're operating on AKS, EKS, GKE, or managing a self-hosted Kubernetes setup, AlertMend's multi-cloud support ensures your clusters are managed efficiently, no matter where they’re hosted.

Seamless Kubernetes Fleet Management

Easily manage your entire Kubernetes fleet with AlertMend’s centralized platform. From a single cluster to hundreds, we provide the tools to maintain visibility and control across all environments.

Easy to Maintain

With automation, customizable workflows, and GitOps support, maintaining your incident management system becomes simple and scalable, saving your team time and reducing errors.

Detailed RCA Information

Provides comprehensive Root Cause Analysis (RCA) information, giving you detailed insights into the underlying causes of incidents. This data helps prevent future issues and is shared through integrated support channels like Slack and Microsoft Teams for collaboration.

Manual Approval Process

Allows teams to manually approve remediation steps through Slack or Microsoft Teams before execution. The approval process is supported by detailed RCA insights, enabling informed, secure decision-making before any actions are taken.
