## Key Metrics for Efficient Kubernetes Cost Management

To effectively manage Kubernetes expenses, it's essential to monitor a range of metrics that provide insights into both resource usage and cost efficiency. The following are some pivotal metrics that alertmend.io tracks:

- **Cost per Pod**: Understanding how much each pod contributes to the overall expense can help identify costly workloads.
- **Cost per Namespace**: By associating costs with specific namespaces, businesses can allocate expenses to teams or projects accurately.
- **CPU and Memory Usage**: These fundamental metrics are crucial for tracking resource consumption and identifying overprovisioned resources.
- **Network Egress**: Monitoring outbound traffic costs is vital, especially for multi-cloud or hybrid deployments.
