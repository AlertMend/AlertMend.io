## Common Challenges in Kubernetes Cost Management

While powerful, Kubernetes environments can present several cost-related challenges:

### Overprovisioning and Underutilization

One of the most common issues is **overprovisioning**, where resources are allocated beyond what is necessary, leading to wasted expenditure. Conversely, underutilization can occur if resources are not scaled down appropriately during periods of low demand.

### Lack of Visibility

Without adequate cost visibility, it becomes challenging to identify areas of inefficiency. This lack of transparency often results in unexpected cloud bills.

### Manual Interventions

Many traditional cost management practices require significant manual intervention, which can lead to human error and increased operational costs.
