## Navigating the Fundamentals of Predictive Analytics in AIOps

Predictive analytics within AIOps involves the strategic use of data, machine learning, and statistical algorithms to predict future outcomes based on historical patterns. ### Core Components of Predictive Analytics

1. **Machine Learning Models**: These models analyze historical data to understand normal operating conditions and identify anomalies that precede incidents.
   
2. **Real-Time Data Ingestion**: Continuous data collection from diverse sources like logs, metrics, and traces allows for dynamic and responsive monitoring.
   
3. **Anomaly Detection**: By spotting deviations from established norms, it flags potential risks early, preventing performance degradation.

4. **Risk Scoring**: Systems are prioritized based on risk scores derived from current performance metrics and historical patterns.
