At its core, a runbook is a detailed collection of procedures and instructions designed to assist IT operations teams in executing routine tasks, incident responses, and system management with precision. ### Categories of Runbook Automation
Runbooks are categorized based on their level of automation:

- **Semi-Automated Runbooks**: Involve minimal human intervention, where tasks can be triggered automatically. - **Fully Automated Runbooks**: Operate independently, executing procedures without any human input, leveraging scripts and software to streamline operations.
