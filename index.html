Welcome to AlertMend
Your SRE and Platform Troubleshooting Companion

Empower your SRE and platform teams with AlertMend, the ultimate tool for diagnosing and resolving issues in any Kubernetes environment. Our Remediation Flows (RF) seamlessly integrate with popular alerting tools to enable automated responses to both pre-defined alerts and custom incidents.


Save up to 85% of your team’s time on issue resolution. Trust AlertMend to help you maintain a robust and resilient Kubernetes infrastructure.


Compatible with AKS, EKS, GKE, and self-hosted Kubernetes.

Technologies

AlertMend seamlessly integrates with leading alerting and monitoring tools such as Alertmanager, Prometheus, and Grafana, allowing you to effortlessly trigger Remediation Flows (RFs) within your Kubernetes stack. In addition, it supports communication platforms like Slack and Microsoft Teams, along with incident management tools like PagerDuty and ServiceNow, enabling real-time notifications and streamlined incident response across your entire ecosystem
